# Quality Analytics: Process Stability, Capability & Cost Optimization

## Project Overview
This project simulates the role of a **Quality Manager** at a newly established manufacturing plant, **Gainsborough Manufacturing Company**. The objective is to ensure the manufacturing process is both **statistically stable and capable**, while **minimizing the total cost of quality** through data-driven decision making.

The project leverages **Statistical Process Control (SPC)** techniques, **process capability analysis**, and **quality cost trade-off evaluation** using a simulation-based environment.

---

## Business Objective
- Maintain process stability using real-time control charts
- Assess whether processes are capable of meeting specifications
- Minimize total cost of quality by balancing:
  - Prevention costs
  - Appraisal costs
  - Internal failure costs
  - External failure costs
